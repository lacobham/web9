Rylee Goerlitz

Rylee is currently enrolled in the Geographic Information Systems: Application Specialist program at Sir Sandford Fleming College- School of Environmental and Natural Resources Sciences and has recently graduated from the Ecosystem Managment Technician Program also at Fleming College. Additionally, she is a graduate of Brock University gaining a BSc in Bioloigcal Sciences. All of these programs have highlighted the importance of GIS technology in conservation, providing precise spatial analyses that allow for the planning and managment of ecosystems. She has experience working with ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online Suite, and AutoCAD. Along with her experience in the enviornmental field, Rylee has volunteered her time creating habitat suitability maps and planting native plants along the Kawartha Lakes shorelines with organizations like Kawartha Land Trust and Kawartha Lake Stewards Association. All these opportunities have helped Rylee develop hands on expereicne in both the field and office.
