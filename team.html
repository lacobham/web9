Meet the Team

The Turtle Crew is a group of Cartographic and Applications Specialists that are passionate about the environment and wildlife, combining this with their skill in GIS to create effective web solutions.

Rylee Goerlitz

Rylee is currently enrolled in the Geographic Information Systems: Application Specialist program at Sir Sandford Fleming College - School of Environmental and Natural Resources Sciences and has recently graduated from the Ecosystem Management Technician Program also at Fleming College. Additionally, she is a graduate of Brock University gaining a BSc in Bioloigcal Sciences. All of these programs have highlighted the importance of GIS technology in conservation, providing precise spatial analyses that allow for the planning and management of ecosystems. She has experience working with ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, and AutoCAD. Along with her experience in the environmental field, Rylee has worked with the Kawartha Conservation Authority. There she further developed her skills in ArcGIS Pro, gained experience surveying dams/bridges, and contributed to the collection of LiDAR data for floodplain mapping. All these opportunities have helped Rylee develop hands-on expereicne in both the field and office.

Laura Cobham

Laura is currently enrolled in the Geographic Information Systems: Application Specialist program at Fleming College, but previously attended Wilfrid Laurier University, graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Geography and Geomatics.
